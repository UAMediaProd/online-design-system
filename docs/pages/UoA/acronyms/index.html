Acronyms

Most universities are littered with acronyms. This list will help you navigate some of the common ones used within the Online Programs.

Acronym

In words

ABLE

Faculty of Arts, Business, Law and Economics

ADEPT

Adelaide Development Program for Educators and Professionals who Teach

BMD

Bachelor of Medical Doctor

CA

Course Author

CRICOS

Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students

DASE

Division of Academic and Student Engagement

DVC

Deputy Vice-Chancellor

DVCA

Deputy Vice-Chancellor Academic

EDX

No acronym - this is the name of the company that is now owned by 2U

EIS

Educational Innovation & Support, part of the LEI structure

GDP

Graduate Diploma in Psychology

GDPA

Graduate Diploma in Psychology (Advanced)

Go8

Group of Eight

HEO

HMS

HoS

Head of School

HSW

Health, Safety and Wellbeing

LEI

Learning Enhancement & Innovation

MBA

Masters of Business Administration

MCY

Masters of Cyber Security

MDS

Masters of Data Science

MOOC

Massive Open Online Courses

OPT

Online Programs Team

OUA

Open Universities Australia

PACE

Professional And Continuing Education

PD

Position Description

PDR

Planning, Development & Review - annual check in for personal and professional development

PVC

Pro-Vice Chancellor

SELTs

Student Experience of Learning and Teaching - student survey at the end of a course

SET

Faculty of Sciences, Engineering, and Technology

SME

Subject Matter Expert

SPP

Strategic Projects and Partnerships - the broader team OPT is in within LEI

SSO

Staff Services Online - system for logging leave and updating details

University of Adelaide

VC
